About Idle Hands

A calm space built around thoughtful, restorative massage therapy.

Hi, i’m Lynn. My background is rooted in movement, collaboration and craft - I’ve spent my career using my hands and working closely with people who do the same. Massage was a natural progression: a slower, more grounded way to stay connected to the body and how we live in it. I understand the physical demands of modern life - the repetition, the intensity, the need for recovery and care. With idle hands, I offer massage searching for a moment to transcend. It’s responsive, down to earth body work, designed to meet you where you are and take you by the hand.
